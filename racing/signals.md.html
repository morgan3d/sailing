In that case, the finish line is marked by the S flag. All Flags ==================================================================== The flags for signaling during racing are the standard international maritime signal flags under the [International Code of Signals (ICS)/INTERCO](https://msi.nga.mil/api/publications/download?key=16694273/SFH00000/Pub102bk.pdf&type=view), plus an orange and a blue flag. However, the flag _interpretation_ differs from the ICS, and the sound signals do not match the Morse code for the flags from ICS. In racing, the Code/Answer flag is referred to as the "Answering Pennant" and abbreviated "AP". The first repeater/Repeat 1 flag is referred to as the "General Recall" flag.
